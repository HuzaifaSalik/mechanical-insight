About Our Services

Professional engineering simulation and design solutions for reliable, efficient, and safe product development

We are a professional engineering simulation and design service provider, specializing in CFD, FEA, and mechanical CAD solutions. Our mission is to help companies, startups, and industrial teams develop reliable, efficient, and safe products through advanced engineering analysis and design validation.

We deliver accurate, industry-grade simulations for structural strength, fluid flow, heat transfer, fatigue life prediction, design optimization, failure analysis, and performance validation across diverse engineering applications.

We support a wide range of industries, including automotive, aerospace, energy systems, pressure vessels, and industrial machinery. From concept development to final performance verification, we work closely with clients to meet engineering standards, safety requirements, and performance targets.

In addition to commercial projects, we actively engage in research collaboration with laboratories, R&D teams, and industrial research centers to support product development, testing, and innovation-driven engineering solutions.
